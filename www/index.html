About Us
Intermittent Fasting is an eating schedule that allows you to choose when you fast and when you eat.
Intermittent Fasting has been shown to help you lose and maintain your weight, give your gut a rest, improve mental clarity and overall health.
Choose the eating plan that works best for you.
FastTrack is here to help you track your fasting windows and overall progress.
